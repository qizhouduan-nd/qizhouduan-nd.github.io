About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Quantitative Psychology at the University of Notre Dame and a fellow in the ND PIER program (Program for Interdisciplinary Educational Research). My research focuses on psychometrics, particularly in response time modeling and differential item functioning. You can find my CV here.

Research Interests

Response Time Modeling

Developing methods to use response time data in psychometric analysis and detection of item biases.

Meta-Analysis

Applying and developing methods for systematic reviews and meta-analyses in educational research.

Differential Item Functioning

Investigating methods for detecting and analyzing item bias in educational and psychological testing.
