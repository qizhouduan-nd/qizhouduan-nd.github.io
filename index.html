Short Bio

My research mainly focuses on response time models in the context of psychometric analysis, looking for ways to utilize response time to either improve parameter esitmation or to detect item biases. Besides response time models, I am interested in both conducting meta-analysis, and methodologies related to meta-analysis. I also have substantive interests in clinical and cognitive research.
